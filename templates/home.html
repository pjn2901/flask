About Us

About Us

Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University (PDPU) is a leading research and educational institution located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India. Established in 2007 by the Government of Gujarat, PDPU has quickly become renowned for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation.

Our Vision

At PDPU, our vision is to be a globally recognized institution that fosters academic excellence, innovation, and societal development. We strive to nurture leaders and professionals who can make significant contributions to their fields and communities.

Our Mission

  • To provide world-class education and research opportunities across various disciplines.
  • To promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.
  • To foster a culture of social responsibility and sustainable development.
  • To collaborate with industry, government, and academia for impactful research and technology transfer.

Academic Programs

PDPU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, humanities, and sciences. Our curriculum is designed to be rigorous, industry-aligned, and intellectually stimulating, preparing students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Research and Innovation

Research is at the heart of PDPU's mission. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge research across diverse areas, including energy, environment, technology, and management. Through collaboration with industry partners and government agencies, we aim to address global challenges and drive innovation for a sustainable future.

Campus Life

The PDPU campus provides a vibrant and inclusive environment for learning, creativity, and personal growth. From state-of-the-art infrastructure to a variety of clubs, sports facilities, and cultural events, students have ample opportunities to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and forge lifelong friendships.

Join Us

Whether you're a prospective student, researcher, faculty member, or industry partner, we invite you to join us in our journey of excellence and impact. Explore our website to learn more about PDPU and discover how you can be a part of our dynamic community.
